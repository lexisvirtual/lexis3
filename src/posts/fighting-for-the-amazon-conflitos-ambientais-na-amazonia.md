**3. BRAZILIAN PITFALLS: STOP TRANSLATING LITERALLY**

* Erro Comum (PT-BR thinking): "I'm going to the jungle to hike."
Forma Natural: "I'm going to hike through the jungle."
* Erro Comum (PT-BR thinking): "The company is trying to pass through the bureaucracy."
Forma Natural: "The company is trying to hack through the bureaucracy."
* Erro Comum (PT-BR thinking): "The indigenous people are fighting for their rights for many years."
