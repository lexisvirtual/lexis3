**ATIVO DE TREINO DE ELITE: "BBC Learning English - 6 Minute English / Why are billionaires building bunkers?"**

**# Título**
Por que bilionários estão construindo bunkers?

**## Vocabulary**

- **Bunker**: um abrigo subterrâneo, geralmente construído para proteger contra ataques ou desastres.
- **Bilionário**: uma pessoa que tem uma grande quantidade de dinheiro.
- **Fortificação**: a ação de fortificar ou tornar mais seguro um local.
- **Apocalipse**: um evento catastrófico que pode causar a destruição do mundo.
- **Sobrevivência**: a capacidade de sobreviver em condições difíceis.

**## Key Structures**

- **Why are... building...?**: Por que estão construindo...
- **They are building... to...**: Eles estão construindo... para...
- **It's a matter of...**: É uma questão de...
- **Some people think that...**: Alguns pensam que...

**## Dialogues**

- **Presenter:** Welcome to 6 Minute English. I'm your presenter, Liz Waid.
- **Liz Waid:** Today, we're talking about billionaires building bunkers. Why are they doing this?
- **Presenter:** That's right. Our guest is Alex Anasan, a journalist who's been investigating this story.
- **Alex Anasan:** Yes, I've been looking into it. It seems that some billionaires are building bunkers as a way to survive an apocalypse.
- **Liz Waid:** An apocalypse? That sounds dramatic.
- **Alex Anasan:** Yes, it is. But some people think that an apocalypse could happen due to climate change, nuclear war or a pandemic.
- **Liz Waid:** That's a scary thought. But what kind of bunkers are we talking about?
- **Alex Anasan:** They're usually underground bunkers with their own air supply, food and water. Some of them even have their own power plants.
- **Liz Waid:** That sounds like a luxury bunker. But is it worth the cost?
- **Alex Anasan:** Well, some people think that it's a matter of survival. If an apocalypse happens, they want to be prepared.
- **Liz Waid:** That's an interesting point. But what about the rest of us? Should we be building our own bunkers?
- **Alex Anasan:** I don't think so. I think it's a bit extreme. But it's an interesting topic to discuss.

### Phrase

- **"It's a matter of survival."**: É uma questão de sobrevivência.
- **"Some people think that..."**: Alguns pensam que...
- **"They are building... to..."**: Eles estão construindo... para...
