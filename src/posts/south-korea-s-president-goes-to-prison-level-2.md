## Vocabulary & Key Structures:
- **Impeachment**: A formal process in which an official is accused of unlawful activity.
- **Corruption**: Dishonest or fraudulent conduct by those in power.
- **Sentence**: The punishment assigned to a defendant found guilty by a court.
- **Prison**: A building where people are legally held as a punishment for a crime.
- **Trial**: A formal examination of evidence in a court.
- **Conviction**: A formal declaration that someone is guilty of a criminal offense.
- **Appeal**: Apply to a higher court for a reversal of the decision of a lower court.
