## Vocabulary & Key Structures:
- **Sinkhole**: A large hole in the ground caused by the collapse of a surface layer. (buraco no solo)
- **Collapse**: To fall down or give way. (desabar)
- **Erosion**: The process by which soil and rock are removed from the Earth's surface. (erosão)
- **Geological**: Relating to the study of the Earth's physical structure. (geológico)
- **Infrastructure**: The basic physical systems of a community. (infraestrutura)
