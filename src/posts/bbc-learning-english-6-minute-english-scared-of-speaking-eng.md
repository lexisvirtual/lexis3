# Scared of Speaking English? Master It with the Lexis 3F Method

## Quick Answer
Overcome your fear of speaking English by practicing regularly, focusing on key phrases, improving fluidity, and understanding the function of language in real-life situations.

## Vocabulary
1. **Fluency** - The ability to speak or write a language easily and accurately.
2. **Confidence** - The feeling or belief that one can rely on someone or something; firm trust.
3. **Pronunciation** - The way in which a word is pronounced.
4. **Intonation** - The rise and fall of the voice in speaking.
5. **Articulation** - The formation of clear and distinct sounds in speech.
6. **Engagement** - The act of being involved with something.
7. **Interaction** - Communication or direct involvement with someone or something.
8. **Nervousness** - The state of being anxious or apprehensive.
9. **Practice** - Repeated exercise in or performance of an activity or skill to acquire or maintain proficiency.
10. **Feedback** - Information about reactions to a product, a person's performance of a task, etc., used as a basis for improvement.

## 3F Training

### Phrase
- **Objective**: Learn key phrases that are commonly used in business settings.
- **Example**: "Could you please clarify that point?"

### Fluidity
- **Objective**: Practice speaking smoothly without unnecessary pauses.
- **Exercise**: Record yourself speaking for one minute on a business topic and listen for areas of improvement.

### Function
- **Objective**: Understand the purpose of language in different contexts.
- **Scenario**: Role-play a business meeting where you need to present a project update.
