## Erros Comuns | Avoiding Amateur Pitfalls
Evite cair em armadilhas comuns de comunicação que podem comprometer sua credibilidade.

| Don't Say | Say Instead |
|-----------|-------------|
| "We have a problem." | "We are addressing a **system failure** with urgency." |
| "I don't know." | "Let me gather more information and get back to you with a solution." |
