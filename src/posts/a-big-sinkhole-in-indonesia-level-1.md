## Vocabulary & Key Structures:
- **Sinkhole**: A large hole in the ground caused by erosion. (buraco)
- **Erosion**: The process by which soil and rock are removed from the Earth's surface. (erosão)
- **Collapse**: To fall down or give way. (colapso)
- **Geological**: Related to the study of the Earth's physical structure. (geológico)
- **Phenomenon**: A fact or situation that is observed to exist. (fenômeno)
