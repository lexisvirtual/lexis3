### The grammar of a pro

* Verb + -ing: used to describe an action that is happening at the moment of speaking (e.g., "I am building a bunker.")
* Phrasal verbs: used to describe an action that has a specific meaning (e.g., "get on with" means to continue doing something)
* Collocations: used to describe a group of words that are often used together (e.g., "take a deep breath")

## 3. BRAZILIAN PITFALLS: STOP TRANSLATING LITERALLY

* Erro Comum (PT-BR thinking): "I am building a bunker for the end of the world."
Forma Natural: "I'm building a bunker to prepare for the apocalypse."
* Erro Comum (PT-BR thinking): "The billionaire has a panic room in his house."
Forma Natural: "The billionaire's mansion has a panic room in case of an emergency."
* Erro Comum (PT-BR thinking): "The doomsday clock is ticking, and we need to do something."
Forma Natural: "The doomsday clock is ticking, and we need to take action."
