## Introduction

As a professional, you know how important it is to communicate effectively in English, especially when dealing with international clients and colleagues. However, mastering business English can be a daunting task, especially when you're not sure where to start.

In this article, we'll explore 50 essential business English terms that every professional should know. We'll cover topics such as business meetings, negotiations, and communication strategies, providing you with the language skills you need to succeed in a globalized business environment.

## 1. Key Terms for Business Meetings

### 1.1 Agenda

* English: "What's on the agenda for today's meeting?"
* Portuguese: "O que está programado para a reunião de hoje?"

### 1.2 Minutes

* English: "Can you take minutes during the meeting?"
* Portuguese: "Pode tomar notas durante a reunião?"

### 1.3 Follow-up

* English: "Can you follow up on the action items from last week's meeting?"
* Portuguese: "Pode seguir o andamento dos itens de ação da reunião da semana passada?"

### 1.4 Action Items

* English: "What are the action items for this project?"
* Portuguese: "Quais são os itens de ação para este projeto?"

### 1.5 Decision-Making

* English: "Who makes the final decision on this project?"
* Portuguese: "Quem toma a decisão final sobre este projeto?"

### 1.6 Feedback

* English: "Can you provide feedback on the presentation?"
* Portuguese: "Pode fornecer feedback sobre a apresentação?"

### 1.7 Task Delegation

* English: "Can you delegate some tasks to your team members?"
* Portuguese: "Pode delegar algumas tarefas para os membros da sua equipe?"

### 1.8 Meeting Cancellation

* English: "I'm afraid the meeting has been cancelled."
* Portuguese: "Lamento, a reunião foi cancelada."

### 1.9 Meeting Rescheduling

* English: "Can we reschedule the meeting for next week?"
* Portuguese: "Pode reschedule a reunião para a próxima semana?"

## 2. Key Terms for Negotiations

### 2.1 Proposal

* English: "We're proposing a new partnership agreement."
* Portuguese: "Estamos propondo um novo acordo de parceria."

### 2.2 Counteroffer

* English: "We're making a counteroffer to your proposal."
* Portuguese: "Estamos fazendo uma contra-proposta à sua proposta."

### 2.3 Price Negotiation

* English: "Can we negotiate the price of this product?"
* Portuguese: "Pode negociar o preço deste produto?"

### 2.4 Contract

* English: "We need to review the contract before signing."
* Portuguese: "Nós precisamos rever o contrato antes de assinar."

### 2.5 Terms and Conditions

* English: "Can you explain the terms and conditions of this contract?"
* Portuguese: "Pode explicar os termos e condições deste contrato?"

### 2.6 Warranty

* English: "The product comes with a one-year warranty."
* Portuguese: "O produto vem com uma garantia de um ano."

### 2.7 Refund Policy

* English: "What is the refund policy for this product?"
* Portuguese: "Qual é a política de devolução para este produto?"

### 2.8 Dispute Resolution

* English: "How do we resolve disputes in this partnership?"
* Portuguese: "Como resolvemos disputas nessa parceria?"

## 3. Key Terms for Communication Strategies

### 3.1 Active Listening

* English: "Can you please repeat that?"
* Portuguese: "Pode repetir isso, por favor?"

### 3.2 Clear Communication

* English: "Can you communicate your expectations clearly?"
* Portuguese: "Pode comunicar suas expectativas claramente?"

### 3.3 Effective Communication

* English: "How can we improve our communication strategy?"
* Portuguese: "Como podemos melhorar nossa estratégia de comunicação?"

### 3.4 Cultural Awareness

* English: "Let's be aware of cultural differences in our communication."
* Portuguese: "Vamos ter em mente as diferenças culturais em nossa comunicação."

### 3.5 Nonverbal Communication

* English: "Your body language is saying something different."
* Portuguese: "Seu linguagem corporal está dizendo algo diferente."

### 3.6 Conflict Resolution

* English: "How do we resolve conflicts in the workplace?"
* Portuguese: "Como resolvemos conflitos no local de trabalho?"

## Conclusion

Mastering 50 essential business English terms is a great starting point for improving your communication skills in a globalized business environment. Remember to practice active listening, clear communication, and cultural awareness to build strong relationships with international clients and colleagues.
